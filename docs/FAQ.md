# GetAutoReply - Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is GetAutoReply?**
A: GetAutoReply is a WhatsApp auto-reply service for Indian small businesses. It automatically responds to customer messages when you're busy, sleeping, or away.

**Q: Who is it for?**
A: Any business that uses WhatsApp to communicate with customers - kirana stores, doctors, salons, tutors, service providers, etc.

**Q: Do I need technical knowledge?**
A: No! Setup is simple. Just enter your business details and create your auto-reply messages. No coding needed.

---

## How It Works

**Q: How does auto-reply work?**
A: You set trigger words (like "timing", "price", "order") and responses. When a customer sends a message containing those words, they get an automatic reply.

**Q: Can I customize the replies?**
A: Yes! You write your own reply messages in Hindi, English, or any language you prefer.

**Q: Does it work 24/7?**
A: Yes! Auto-replies work round the clock, even when you're sleeping or busy.

**Q: Will customers know it's an auto-reply?**
A: That's up to you. You can make replies sound natural or clearly indicate they're automatic.

## Technical

**Q: Do I need WhatsApp Business?**
A: Yes, you need a WhatsApp Business account (free from Google Play/App Store).

**Q: Will it affect my regular WhatsApp chats?**
A: No, it only works with your WhatsApp Business number, not personal WhatsApp.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes, we don't store your actual messages. We only process trigger words to send replies.

**Q: What if I exceed my message limit?**
A: Auto-replies pause until next month, or you can upgrade your plan.
